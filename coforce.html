What is it about:

Building the MVP for a mobile app for Coforce start-up. Our task was to create a matching tool for core team members for start-ups. The matching process is based on a passion for all team members to share and complementary skills. After setting up your profile with your personal information, location, interests and hard/ soft skills, the app will suggest you projects/ start-ups that only compatible with your profile.

The challenging part of this project was to collaborate with real stakeholders, meet all their needs while following the design thinking process in less than 2 weeks.
