About me
Hi, my name is Rony Rom!

I was born and raised in Haifa, Israel (except for the 3 years we lived in New Jersey, NY in the early 90’s). Before becoming a UX/UI Designer, I worked as an Optometrist (the person who checks your vision and tells you that you need glasses).

Why UX/UI Design?

Technology, computers, and other geeky stuff is something I always had an affinity for. As the one who was always fixing things around, it was just a matter of time for me to leave my previous profession and find myself doing what I enjoy the most: coming up with simple techy solutions for daily struggles.
I design pleasurable User Experiences, consistent User Interfaces, and smart interactions.

Currently...

…I’m teaching UX/UI Design at Ironhack, Berlin which allows me to develop my skills and enrich my knowledge in design. I'm now looking for my next challenge as I want to keep on learning and growing. What excites me is giving the users a great experience through smooth and clean interaction design.

Why Berlin?

Well, I arrived not so long ago for studies and stayed because of a work opportunity. During this time I found out that I enjoy living in Berlin and generally in Europe. Even though Berlin is great, I'm open to relocate and discover new places.

Other things I like:

Traveling & hiking, photography, Crossfit, bouldering, cooking, baking & food in general.
